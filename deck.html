ENTRY-LEVEL ROLES

Deckhand

Responsibilities: Assist with docking, cargo loading, safety checks, and basic ship maintenance.

Salary: €1,500–€2,500/month.

MID-LEVEL ROLES

Able Seaman (AB)

Responsibilities: Perform navigation, safety, and maintenance tasks under supervision.

Salary: €2,500–€3,500/month.

Electrician

Responsibilities: Maintain and repair electrical systems on the ferry.

Salary: €2,500–€4,000/month.

SPECIALIZED ROLES

Engineer (Third, Second, or Chief)

Responsibilities: Oversee engine operations, troubleshoot mechanical issues, and ensure ferry safety.

Salary: €3,500–€8,000/month (depending on rank).

Captain

Responsibilities: Navigate the ferry, oversee operations, and ensure passenger and crew safety.

Salary: €5,000–€15,000/month.
