SUPPORT ROLES

Land-Based and Administrative

"The magic of the voyage depends on a hundred small details working perfectly behind the scenes. That’s where we come in—and we take pride in being invisible but essential"

ENTRY-LEVEL ROLES

Ticketing Agent

Responsibilities: Assist passengers, serve food and drinks, clean cabins, and handle customer inquiries.

Salary: €1,200–€2,000/month.

Port Assistant

Responsibilities: Help with passenger boarding, cargo handling, and port coordination.


Salary: €1,200–€1,800/month.

MID-LEVEL ROLES

Operations Coordinator

Responsibilities: Schedule ferry departures, manage logistics, and ensure compliance with regulations.

Salary: €2,500–€4,000/month.

Marketing Executive

Responsibilities: Promote ferry services, develop campaigns, and analyze customer feedback.


Salary: €2,500–€4,500/month.

SPECIALIZED ROLES

Marine Superintendent

Responsibilities: Oversee fleet operations, ensure regulatory compliance, and manage crew training.

Salary: €5,000–€10,000/month.
