ONBOARD OPERATIONS

CUSTOMER-FACING ROLES

APPLY NOW

"“Every day I step onto the deck, I know I’m helping create unforgettable memories. Whether it's guiding a guest to their suite or simply sharing a laugh, I’m proud to be the warm welcome that sets the tone for their journey."

– Rosie Miller, Stewardess

ENTRY-LEVEL ROLES

Steward/Stewardess

Responsibilities: Assist passengers, serve food and drinks, clean cabins, and handle customer inquiries.

Salary: €1,200–€2,000/month.

Retail Assistant

Responsibilities: Work in onboard shops, sell duty-free items, and manage inventory.


Salary: €1,200–€1,800/month.

MID-LEVEL ROLES

Guest Relations Officer

Responsibilities: Address passenger concerns, coordinate special services, and oversee customer experience.

Salary: €2,000–€3,500/month.

Retail Assistant

Responsibilities: Manage dining services, supervise stewards, and ensure high-quality food and service.


Salary: €2,000–€3,000/month.

SPECIALIZED ROLES

Guest Relations Officer

Responsibilities: Address passenger concerns, coordinate special services, and oversee customer experience.

Salary: €2,000–€3,500/month.
